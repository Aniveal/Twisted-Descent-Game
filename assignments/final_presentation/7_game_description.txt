Twisted Descent is an action-packed cave exploration game. Play as Theseus and venture deep into the Minotaur's cave, but beware of danger! When confronting dangerous Cyclops and Minotaur, use the mighty string of Ariadne to slam your enemies into walls, pull them into cliffs, or hurdle amphoras at them.

However, even the most accomplished warrior may find their limits. How deep into the cave are you willing to push yourself?
